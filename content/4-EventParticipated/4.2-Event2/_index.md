# Summary Report: “AWS: Enterprise Cloud Architectures and Industry Application featuring Cloud Kinetics & Renova Cloud”

### Event Objectives

- Share current career trends in Cloud Computing and the IT industry in the era of AI.
- Provide practical insights from industry experts regarding recruitment expectations for students, interns, and fresh graduates.
- Introduce the fundamental knowledge required for Data Engineer and Cloud Engineer career paths.
- Share experiences on personal development, career growth, and adapting to the rapid advancement of AI.

### Speakers

- **Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung** – Head of Solutions Architect in Vietnam & Cambodia, Amazon Web Services
- **Mr. Vinh Banh** – Senior Data Engineer, Renova Cloud
- **Ms. Nhu Tran** – Account Manager, Amazon Web Services
- **Mr. Khang Nguyen** – Solutions Architect, Cloud Kinetics

### Key Highlights

#### Job Market & Career Trends (Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung)

The speaker provided an overview of today's IT job market and the changes students should prepare for in order to remain competitive.

- **Rising recruitment expectations:** Companies now expect interns and fresh graduates to contribute to real projects much sooner instead of requiring extensive initial training.
- **Focus on domains rather than job titles:** Instead of aiming only to become a Frontend, Backend, or DevOps developer, students should choose long-term career domains such as Cloud, AI, Data, or Cybersecurity.
- **Growing Cloud market in Vietnam:** Digital transformation continues to increase the demand for cloud technologies, making Cloud Computing one of the most promising career fields.
- **Be proactive in seeking opportunities:** Since not every company hires interns or fresh graduates, students should actively explore opportunities across different companies and industries.
- **Build professional networking:** Participating in technology communities, workshops, and networking events increases opportunities for referrals and career development.
- **AI creates new career opportunities:** AI is transforming the way software is developed rather than eliminating jobs. Faster software development also leads to higher demand for new digital products and additional employment opportunities.

#### Becoming a Data Engineer (Mr. Vinh Banh)

Mr. Vinh Banh shared the knowledge and skills students should prepare before pursuing a career in Data Engineering.

- **Master the fundamentals:** Before learning advanced technologies, students should build strong foundations in **Database & Data Modeling**, **APIs**, **Data Structures & Algorithms**, and **Distributed Systems**.
- **Bridging the gap between education and industry:** University courses provide theoretical understanding, while real-world projects require practical problem-solving, teamwork, and engineering processes.
- **Personal projects vs. enterprise projects:** Personal projects help build technical skills and portfolios but differ significantly from enterprise systems in terms of scale, data volume, development processes, and quality requirements.
- **Commit to continuous learning:** Data technologies evolve rapidly, making continuous self-learning and hands-on practice essential for long-term career growth.

#### Personal Growth & Overcoming Mental Barriers (Ms. Nhu Tran)

Ms. Nhu Tran emphasized that career success depends not only on technical expertise but also on mindset and self-confidence.

- **Avoid comparing yourself with others:** Everyone has different strengths, learning speeds, and career journeys. Constant comparison only creates unnecessary pressure.
- **Everyone is unique:** Instead of trying to become someone else, individuals should focus on developing their own strengths and career paths.
- **Overcome the fear of being judged:** Many students hesitate to ask questions or express ideas because they fear criticism. Stepping outside the comfort zone leads to greater growth.
- **View feedback positively:** Constructive criticism should be seen as an opportunity to improve rather than something negative.
- **Build confidence through action:** Confidence is developed through continuous learning, practice, and gradually overcoming challenges.

#### AI & Strong Fundamentals in the Modern Era (Mr. Khang Nguyen)

Mr. Khang Nguyen discussed how AI is transforming the technology industry while highlighting the importance of strong engineering fundamentals.

- **AI is changing the way we work:** AI accelerates coding, data analysis, and information retrieval, allowing developers to become more productive.
- **Fundamental knowledge remains essential:** Although AI is a powerful assistant, engineers still need strong technical foundations to validate AI-generated results and avoid misinformation.
- **Use AI as a supporting tool:** AI should enhance human productivity rather than replace critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
- **Lifelong learning is necessary:** Continuously updating technical knowledge is essential to keep pace with the rapidly evolving IT industry.
- **Recruitment criteria beyond technical skills:** Employers also evaluate candidates based on attitude, practical experience, learning ability, and long-term growth potential.
