# Feedback and Suggestions

During my internship at **First Cloud AI Journey**, I gained valuable hands-on experience and learned many practical skills related to AWS and cloud computing. The internship provided a great opportunity to apply academic knowledge in a real working environment while improving both my technical and professional skills.

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The working environment was friendly, supportive, and encouraging. Everyone was approachable and willing to help whenever I encountered difficulties, making it easy for interns to adapt and learn.

### 2. Mentor and Admin Team Support

The mentors provided clear guidance, constructive feedback, and practical advice throughout the internship. The admin team was also responsive and helpful in handling schedules, documents, and internship-related activities.

### 3. Relevance to My Major

The internship tasks were closely related to my Computer Science background, especially in cloud computing, AWS services, and system deployment. This allowed me to strengthen my technical foundation while gaining practical experience.

### 4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities

The program gave me the opportunity to explore various AWS services, design cloud architectures, deploy applications, and improve my research and problem-solving skills. Writing technical blogs also helped enhance my ability to communicate technical concepts.

### 5. Culture and Teamwork

The team maintained a positive and collaborative working culture. Everyone respected each other, shared knowledge openly, and created an environment where interns felt comfortable asking questions and learning.

### 6. Internship Program

The internship offered a well-structured learning roadmap, useful learning materials, and practical assignments. These resources helped me gradually build both technical knowledge and real-world experience.
